Characters

Patsy Leon

The Court King of Westbond
Affiliation: Nova Watch Supporter
Roles: Rapper, Basketball Player

Patsy Leon is one of Westbond City’s loudest voices — a rapper whose lyrics attack Guardian corruption and a basketball star whose games unite the city. His hatred for the Guardians is unmatched, and he uses his influence to support Nova Watch operations through music, crowds, and underground messaging.

Alan Jez

The Robin Hood of Guardia
Affiliation: Neutral (Supports Westbond)
Roles: Robber, Smuggler

Alan Jez is infamous for robbing Guardia City’s wealthy elites and secretly funneling the money back into Westbond’s poorest districts. He never harms civilians and never keeps a dollar for himself. To Guardia he’s a criminal — to Westbond he’s a hero.

Delmar Zack

The Laser Sentinel
Affiliation: Nova Watch Member
Roles: Soldier, City Defender

Delmar Zack is one of Nova Watch’s most disciplined soldiers. Armed with a high‑precision laser pistol, he patrols Westbond’s borders with unwavering seriousness. He believes the city must be protected at all costs — from Seron Darkness, Guardian oppression, and any threat that crosses the line.
