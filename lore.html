Lore

Guardia City Bank Robbery

When Alan Jez planned the largest heist Westbond had ever seen, he knew he couldn’t do it alone. He gathered Patsy Leon and Delmar Zack for a direct strike against Guardia City and the Guardians themselves.

The trio stormed the bank with precision — Alan cracking the vault, Patsy controlling the crowd, and Delmar holding the line with his laser pistol. Their escape was nearly cut short when Kole Rogue (The Arsonist), a Guardian member, confronted them.

Delmar didn’t hesitate. He stunned Kole with a clean laser shot and coldly told him he was “a mistake of life,” before dragging Patsy and Alan out of the burning lobby.

The money was delivered to Westbond’s poorest districts by nightfall. The Guardians called it terrorism. Westbond called it justice.

The Guardia City Vandalism

After the bank heist, the trio struck again — this time targeting Seron’s Statue in the heart of Guardia City. Alan Jez, Patsy Leon, and Delmar Zack planned to vandalize the monument as a symbolic blow against Seron Darkness and the Guardians’ propaganda.

But the plan collapsed instantly when Tom Letanio appeared. With his indigo energy blazing, Tom unleashed a barrage of Indigo Fist strikes that nearly killed all three.

Delmar managed to pull Patsy and Alan out of the blast zone, escaping through the narrow alleys of Guardia. Patsy spent the entire escape ranting about Tom Letanio — swearing he hated him more than any Guardian alive.

The statue remained untouched. But the message was still sent: Westbond wasn’t afraid.
