Nova Watch Lives In A Hostile City

In Westbond City RP, the spotlight falls on the everyday and extraordinary lives of Nova Watch Supporters and Nova Watch Members as they navigate a city carved into the larger conflict of The Rise Of Seron.

Their stories unfold in streets overshadowed by the hostile gaze of the Guardians and the creeping influence of Seron Darkness—where loyalty, survival, and quiet rebellion collide in the alleys, towers, and hidden networks of Westbond.

Nova Watch Perspective

Follow Nova Watch Members and Supporters as they balance public duty, underground alliances, and personal sacrifice. Every patrol, meeting, and whispered conversation reveals how far they will go to protect Westbond from the shadows pressing in.

Hostile Forces

The Guardians’ rigid control and the insidious spread of Seron Darkness shape the city’s tension. Westbond becomes a battleground of ideology and fear, where Nova Watch must decide when to stand visible—and when to move unseen.
